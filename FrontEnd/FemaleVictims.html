Female Victims

 This graph is categorizing female victims by their age and week in the year when the crime occurred. LA's demographics are a majority female. Millenial women are the most victimized group consistently.

 The trend is easily explained by the fact that millenials are the highest population in LA, as well as the largest generation, recently surpassing the baby boomers in number. Although it is interesting to see that the baby boomers are not highly victimized usually despite being the second largest generation. Older people face a lot of difficulties, although they are usually more systemic with healthcare or housing rather than criminal.
