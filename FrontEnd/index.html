Crime Data Trends

 Our project focuses on a set of crime data from Los Angeles from the years 2019 to present. We intend to use this data to form a database and website that will show the trends in crimes in Los Angeles. During 2020, many individuals realized the implicit biases and injustices that reside with United States law enforcement. Our project not only aims to visualize and present trends relating to crime in general, but also the issues that may exist in our justice system.

 We will present trends pertaining to arrests by race, theft by season, gun crime by month, female victims by age group, and crime rates by location. We feel that analyzing these trends can help to highlight the issues persisting towards these groups and inspire change.
