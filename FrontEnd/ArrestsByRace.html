Arrests By Race

 This trend is the number of arrests made by month. The data is then categorized into four different race classifications. Black, White, Latine, and Asian. It is important to note that the race provided is the race of the victim of the recorded crime.

 Each police station is tied connected to a certain area in LA. For example, Police Station 1 oversees the Central Area. In most areas, the highest victimized race is Hispanic people, likely due to the White Hispanics being the largest ethnic group in LA. If another race breaks this pattern, it is significant

 While analyzing this trend, pay close attention to Police Stations 3, 12, and 18. These areas form Southern LA, an area with a history of racial violence starting in the 1950s. Black people are still highly victimized in these areas as seen in the trend chart.
